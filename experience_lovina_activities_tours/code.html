Beyond the black sand beaches of Lovina lies a world of quiet wonders, ancient waterfalls, and the silent rhythm of the Indian Ocean.

Discovering the Soul of Buleleng

Lovina is not just a destination; it is a sanctuary for those seeking the authentic Bali. Located in the Buleleng Regency, North Bali offers a stark, beautiful contrast to the bustling south. Here, luxury is measured in sunrise dolphin sightings, the mist of hidden waterfalls, and the unhurried pace of coastal life.

At Bhumi Lovina Residence, we curate bespoke journeys that connect you with the heritage and natural brilliance of this region. Whether you are trekking through the lush highlands of Munduk or diving into the coral gardens of Menjangan, our concierge team ensures every moment is tailored to your serenity.

A Local Guide to North Bali Wonders

When is the best time to see Dolphins in Lovina?

The best time for dolphin watching in Lovina is during the dry season, from April to October. However, dolphins are present year-round. We recommend heading out at 5:45 AM to catch the first light of dawn as the sea is typically at its calmest, providing the clearest visibility.

Navigating the Sekumpul Waterfall Hike

Sekumpul is not a simple walk; it requires a moderate level of fitness as there are many steps down to the base. We highly recommend using a local guide from the village to support the community and ensure safety. The mist from the falls is intense—we provide waterproof gear for all our guests visiting the falls.

Sustainable Tourism in North Bali

Bhumi Lovina is committed to preserving the natural beauty of Buleleng. We partner with local boatmen who adhere to eco-friendly practices and support reef restoration projects. By choosing our tours, you contribute directly to the conservation of the Lovina coastline.
